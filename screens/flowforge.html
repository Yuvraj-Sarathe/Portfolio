Problem & solution

! Problem

Todo apps lack real sync

Most todo applications are either too simple (local-only, no sync) or too complex (full project management suites). Users need a middle ground — a clean, fast todo list that syncs across devices without requiring a paid subscription or complicated setup. Authentication should be one tap, not another signup form.

→ Solution

Firebase-powered task management

FlowForge combines Firebase's real-time database with Google Login authentication for a frictionless experience. Sign in with your Google account and start managing tasks instantly. Todos sync in real-time across all your devices. Clean, focused UI with drag-and-drop prioritization, due dates, and categories — everything a productive person needs, nothing they don't.
